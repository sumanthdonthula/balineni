Balineni Srinivas Reddy

Balineni Srinivasa Reddy (born 12 December 1964, Ongole, Andhra Pradesh, India) is an Indian politician and Minister for Energy, forest & environment, science & technology in the state of Andhra Pradesh Member of the Legislative Assembly from Ongole.Balineni Srinivasa Reddy is a five times Member of Legislative Assembly (MLA) from Ongole, Andhra Pradesh.He is member of the YSR Congress Party and has represented the Ongole constituency for six consecutive terms.He also served as Minister of Mines & Geology, Handlooms & Textiles, Spinning Mills, Small Scale Industries in YSR 2nd cabinet in the year 2009.
